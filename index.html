My Mission

Artificial intelligence is changing how humanity creates value. Yet while much of the conversation focuses on digital products, content generation, and software, some of the most important opportunities lie in the physical world — in factories, supply chains, production systems, and operations.

Over decades, companies have built extraordinary expertise in planning, manufacturing, and execution. These systems are complex, constraint-driven, and deeply human. AI doesn't replace that — it amplifies it.

I believe AI has the potential to make operational knowledge more accessible, decisions more informed, and execution more effective.

Building meaningful AI for industrial domains requires more than technical expertise. It requires process understanding, operational thinking, and a deep appreciation for how real-world systems work — where physical constraints, accumulated knowledge, and human judgment all matter.

My mission is to help build the bridge between operations and AI.

Europe has built its prosperity on engineering and operational excellence. I believe the next leap in competitiveness will come from combining these strengths with artificial intelligence — systems that perceive, reason, and act in the physical world.

Contributing to that future is what drives my work.

That's why I'm currently spending a lot of time exploring AI in Operations — working on projects that genuinely excite me, sharing them here, and staying open to feedback or collaboration. If something resonates, feel free to reach out. 😊
